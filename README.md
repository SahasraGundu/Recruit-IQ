# RecruitIQ – AI-Powered Recruitment Intelligence Platform

RecruitIQ is an AI-driven recruitment platform designed to simplify and accelerate the hiring process. The platform helps recruiters manage hiring drives, screen resumes, rank candidates, generate interview insights, track recruitment analytics, and automate candidate communication from a single dashboard.

Built with a recruiter-first approach, RecruitIQ reduces manual screening effort and enables data-driven hiring decisions through AI-powered candidate evaluation and analytics.

---

## Problem Statement

Recruiters often spend significant time manually reviewing resumes, comparing candidates, generating interview questions, and communicating hiring decisions. As the number of applicants increases, the process becomes slow, repetitive, and difficult to scale.

RecruitIQ addresses these challenges by automating key stages of the recruitment workflow using AI.

---

## Key Features

### AI Resume Screening

* Upload and analyze multiple resumes at once.
* Supports both PDF and DOCX resume formats.
* Extracts candidate information automatically.

### Candidate Matching & Ranking

* Compares candidate profiles against job requirements.
* Generates AI-powered match scores.
* Ranks candidates based on suitability.

### AI Recommendations

* Highlights candidate strengths and weaknesses.
* Provides hiring recommendations and risk analysis.
* Generates role-specific interview questions.

### Recruitment Analytics

* Visual dashboards for recruitment insights.
* Hiring trends and candidate distribution analysis.
* Performance tracking across recruitment drives.

### Diversity & Bias Insights

* Provides diversity-focused hiring analytics.
* Helps recruiters monitor fairness and inclusivity.

### Automated Email Communication

* Send hiring status updates directly from the platform.
* Supports selection and rejection notifications.
* Reduces manual recruiter effort.

### Report Generation

* Export recruitment reports in PDF format.
* Generate Excel reports for candidate analysis and tracking.

### Recruiter Chat Assistant

* AI-powered recruiter assistant for candidate-related queries.
* Enables natural language interaction with recruitment data.

## Workflow

1. Recruiter creates a hiring drive.
2. Job description and hiring requirements are added.
3. Candidate resumes are uploaded.
4. AI analyzes and scores each candidate.
5. Candidates are ranked automatically.
6. Recruiters review insights and interview recommendations.
7. Reports are generated.
8. Candidate status updates are communicated through email.
